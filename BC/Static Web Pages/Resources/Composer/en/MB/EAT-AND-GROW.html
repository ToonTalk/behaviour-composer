I eat the food on my patch of ground and grow bigger.

Begin micro-behaviour

EAT-AND-GROW

Begin NetLogo code: 
do-after .02
   [do-every delta-t
       [let this-patch patch my-x my-y 
        let food-availability [patches-food-availability] of this-patch
        let amount-eaten minimum my-maximum-consumption-rate food-availability 
        set my-next-size my-next-size + amount-eaten
        ask this-patch [set patches-food-availability 
                            food-availability - amount-eaten]]
End NetLogo code

How this works

This is delayed by .02 time units in order to cause this phase to happen after the agents move and before they might reproduce. On every time unit it computes the minimum of the food available where the agent is and the agent's maximum consumption rate. The agent's size is increased by the amount eaten and the patch's available food is decremented by that amount.
