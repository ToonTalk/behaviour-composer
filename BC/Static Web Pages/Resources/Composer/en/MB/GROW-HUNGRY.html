I repeatedly increase my hunger by 1 unit.

Begin micro-behaviour

GROW-HUNGRY

Begin NetLogo code: 
do-every delta-t
   [set my-hunger my-hunger + 1]
End NetLogo code

Variants

Any attribute can be repeatedly changed by the desired amount in a similar manner. It need not be defined to happen every "tick", i.e. delta-t seconds.

Related Micro-behaviours

GROW-OLDER is another way of defining an attribute (age in this case) that increases with time.

How this works

This schedules the increase of the hunger attribute by 1 unit to happen every delta-t seconds.
