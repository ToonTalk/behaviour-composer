I turn towards a goal.

Begin micro-behaviour

TURN-TOWARDS-ANOTHER

Begin NetLogo code: 
do-every delta-t
   [let desired-heading heading-towards-another the-other
    let desired-turn desired-heading - my-heading
    turn-right desired-turn]
End NetLogo code

Variants

the-other is a variable referring to another individual. You can replace it an expression such as any-of-kind "Prototype1". You may want to introduce a maximum turning speed.

Related Micro-behaviours

MOVE-FORWARD can be used together with this to move towards a goal. TURN-TOWARDS-GOAL turns me towards a point.

How this works

I compute the angle from where I am to the x and y coordinates of other. I turn towards the goal by the difference between my desired-heading and my-heading.
