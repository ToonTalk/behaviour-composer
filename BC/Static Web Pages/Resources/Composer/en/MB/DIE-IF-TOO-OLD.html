I die if I get too old.  

Begin micro-behaviour

DIE-IF-TOO-OLD

Begin NetLogo code: 
do-after .001
   [do-every delta-t
       [do-if my-age > my-life-expectancy
           [add-behaviour DIE]]]
End NetLogo code

Variants

This follows the model in [1] but a simpler and more realistic approach is to use a probability of dying every delta-t time units. The probability itself could depend upon the age for even more realism.

Related Micro-behaviours

This adds the DIE micro-behaviour if my age is too great. my-life-expectancy is initialised by INITIALISE-LIFETIME and my-age is updated by AGE. DIE-IF-NO-SUGAR is another way to die.

How this works

After a delay of .001 time units to ensure that the aging behaviour AGE has finished, this repeatedly checks if my age exceeds my life expectancy and if so adds the DIE micro-behaviour.
