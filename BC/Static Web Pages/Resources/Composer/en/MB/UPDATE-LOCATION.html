I move forward at a constant speed.

Begin micro-behaviour

UPDATE-LOCATION

Begin NetLogo code: 
do-every delta-t
   [go-forward 1 * delta-t]
End NetLogo code

Variants

The 1 is the speed per second of this agent. It can be changed to any other value.

Related Micro-behaviours

Any behaviour that changes my heading works together with this to produce non-linear motion, for example TURN-IN-DIRECTION-AT-MAXIMUM-SPEED. UPDATE-LOCATION-DELAYED shows how to delay the scheduling of this micro-behaviour.

How this works

This moves the object in the direction of its current heading by a constant amount on each cycle.
