I repeatedly turn left or right and move forward.

Begin micro-behaviour

WANDER-RANDOMLY-BY-TURNING

Begin NetLogo code: 
add-behaviours [TURN-RANDOMLY MOVE-FORWARD]
End NetLogo code

Related Micro-behaviours

This combines TURN-RANDOMLY and MOVE-FORWARD to create a wandering behaviour. WANDER-RANDOMLY-IN-COMPASS-DIRECTIONS moves up, down, left, or right rather than moving forward while turning a random amount.

How this works

The TURN-RANDOMLY changes my heading repeatedly while the MOVE-FORWARD micro-behaviours makes me move forward in the direction I'm headed.
