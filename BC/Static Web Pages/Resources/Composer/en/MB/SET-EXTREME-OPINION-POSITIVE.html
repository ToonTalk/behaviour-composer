I initialise my opinion to a negative extreme.

Begin micro-behaviour

SET-EXTREME-OPINION-POSITIVE

Begin NetLogo code: 
do-at-setup
   [set my-opinion 1]
End NetLogo code

Variants

You can initialise the my-opinion attribute to any value.

Related Micro-behaviours

SET-EXTREME-OPINION-NEGATIVE initialise my-opinion to the opposite extreme. This attribute is used by RELATIVE-AGREEMENT. SET-RANDOM-OPINION, SET-OPINION-UNCERTAINTY, and SET-CERTAIN-OPINION to create a population with a variety of opinions and certainties.

How this works

It sets the attribute my-opinion to 1 as was done in the model described in [1].

History

This was implemented by Ken Kahn.

Footnotes

[1] An implementation of "How can extremism prevail? A study based on the relative agreement interaction model" by Guillaume Deffuant, Frédéric Amblard, Gérard Weisbuch and Thierry Faure (2002); Journal of Artificial Societies and Social Simulation vol. 5, no. 4; <http://jasss.soc.surrey.ac.uk/5/4/1.html#2.3>
