I postpone growing sugar back until agents are finished collecting it.

Begin micro-behaviour

GROW-SUGAR-SEASONALLY-DELAYED

Begin NetLogo code: 
do-after .003
   [add-behaviour GROW-SUGAR-SEASONALLY]
End NetLogo code

Related Micro-behaviours

This relies upon GROW-SUGAR-SEASONALLY to do the real work.

How this works

This starts the repeating process of growing sugar .003 time units late to ensure that the agents have completed collecting sugar with COLLECT-SUGAR.
