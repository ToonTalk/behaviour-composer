I repeatedly either move one unit to the left, right, or stay put.

Begin micro-behaviour

WANDER-HORIZONTALLY

Begin NetLogo code: 
substitute-text-area-for cycle-duration 1                      
          
do-every cycle-duration
   [set my-next-x my-next-x + random-integer-between -1 1]
End NetLogo code

Variants

An interesting variant is to use instead random-number-between -1 1 to generate fractional movements. Feel free to change the range to something other than -1 to 1.

Related Micro-behaviours

WANDER-VERTICALLY differs from this micro-behaviour in that it moves vertically instead of horizontally. WANDER-RANDOMLY-IN-COMPASS-DIRECTIONS combines the two micro-behaviours.

How this works

This updates my horizontal position by randomly either adding 1 or -1 to the current horizontal position.
