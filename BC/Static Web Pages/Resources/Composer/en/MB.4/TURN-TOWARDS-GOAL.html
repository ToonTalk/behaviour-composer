I turn towards a goal.

Begin micro-behaviour

TURN-TOWARDS-GOAL

Begin NetLogo code: 
substitute-text-area-for columns: 56 rows: 2 the-goal 5 5 
substitute-text-area-for columns: 71 cycle-duration 1
substitute-text-area-for columns: 65 turn-expression desired-turn
do-every cycle-duration
   [let desired-heading 
        heading-towards the-goal
    let desired-turn desired-heading - my-heading
    turn-right turn-expression]
End NetLogo code

Variants

The 5 5 represents a goal to the lower right. You can replace it with an attribute or the result of some computation (e.g. a random distribution). You may want to introduce a maximum turning speed by replacing desired-turn with within-range canonical-heading desired-turn -30 30 (this makes the angle turned be between -30 and 30).

Related Micro-behaviours

MOVE-FORWARD can be used together with this to move towards a goal. TURN-TOWARDS-ANOTHER turns me towards another individual.

How this works

I computes the angle from where I am to the x and y coordinates. I turn towards the goal by the difference between my desired-heading and my-heading.
