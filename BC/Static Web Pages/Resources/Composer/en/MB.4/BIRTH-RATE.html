I give birth with some probability.

Begin micro-behaviour

BIRTH-RATE

Begin NetLogo code: 
substitute-text-area-for cycle-duration 1                      
                 
substitute-text-area-for number-of-copies 1       
do-every cycle-duration
   [do-with-probability reciprocal the-average-life-expectancy
       [add-copies number-of-copies 
            list-of-micro-behaviours "Additional behaviours" [NEW-BORN-INITIAL-STATE.html POSSIBLY-VACCINATE-NEW-BORN.html]]]
End NetLogo code

Related Micro-behaviours

CREATE-DEATH-RATE-SLIDER sets the-average-life-expectancy. DEATH-RATE works in a similar manner to this micro-behaviour.

How this works

This computes the odds of giving birth in any week and uses it to determine whether I create a copy of myself initialised by NEW-BORN-INITIAL-STATE and POSSIBLY-VACCINATE-NEW-BORN.
