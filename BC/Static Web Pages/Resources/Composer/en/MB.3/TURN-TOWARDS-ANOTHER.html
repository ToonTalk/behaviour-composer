I turn towards another agent.

Begin micro-behaviour

TURN-TOWARDS-ANOTHER

Begin NetLogo code: 
substitute-text-area-for expression-for-other the-other                             
substitute-text-area-for cycle-duration 1                      
          
do-every cycle-duration
   [let desired-heading heading-towards-another expression-for-other
    let desired-turn desired-heading - my-heading
    turn-right desired-turn]
End NetLogo code

Variants

the-other is a variable referring to another individual. You can replace it an expression such as any-of-kind "Prototype1". You may want to introduce a maximum turning speed.

Related Micro-behaviours

MOVE-FORWARD can be used together with either:

TURN-TOWARDS-GOAL -- I turn towards a goal

TURN-TOWARDS-ANOTHER -- I turn towards another individual

to move towards a goal or another.

MOVE-TOWARDS-ANOTHER -- moves towards another moving only in horizontal or vertical steps.

How this works

I compute the angle from where I am to the location of the-other. I turn towards the goal by the difference between my desired-heading and my-heading.
