I draw a line to the person that infected me.

Begin micro-behaviour

DISPLAY-LINE-OF-INFECTION

Begin NetLogo code: 
substitute-text-area-for line-of-infection-color white   
when [my-state = "infected" and is-object? my-last-encounter]
     [draw-line self my-last-encounter line-of-infection-color
      ask self [set hidden? false]
      ask my-last-encounter [set hidden? false]]
End NetLogo code

Variants

You can change white to another colour.

Related Micro-behaviours

This behaviour relies upon POSSIBLE-INFECTION to spread the infection.

How this works

This checks if I'm infected and I know who I last encountered (i.e. the one who infected me) and then draws a white line between us. If either of us have been hidden (because we are dead) we are made visible.
