I repeatedly increase my hunger by 1 unit.

Begin micro-behaviour

GROW-HUNGRY

Begin NetLogo code: 
substitute-text-area-for cycle-duration 1                      
          
do-every cycle-duration
   [set my-hunger my-hunger + 1]
End NetLogo code

Variants

Any attribute can be repeatedly changed by the desired amount in a similar manner. The 1 can be changed to change the frequency this run.

Related Micro-behaviours

GROW-OLDER is another way of defining an attribute (age in this case) that increases with time.

How this works

This schedules the increase of the hunger attribute by 1 unit to happen every second.
