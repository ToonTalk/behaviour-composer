I repeatedly turn right 10 degrees.

Begin micro-behaviour

TURN-REPEATEDLY

Begin NetLogo code: 
substitute-text-area-for rotation-angle 10                            
substitute-text-area-for cycle-duration 1                             
          
do-every cycle-duration
   [turn-right rotation-angle]
End NetLogo code

Variants

It is safe to change the 10  to any other value or replace it with the value of a parameter (see DEFINE-PARAMETER).

Related Micro-behaviours

TURN-RANDOMLY turns back and forth a random amount.

How this works

This schedules a turn of 10 degree to occur every second.

History

TURN-REPEATEDLY was implemented by Ken Kahn.
