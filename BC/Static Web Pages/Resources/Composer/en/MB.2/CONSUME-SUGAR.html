I consume sugar at my rate of metabolism.

Begin micro-behaviour

CONSUME-SUGAR

Begin NetLogo code: 
do-every 1
   [set my-sugar my-sugar - (my-sugar-consumption * delta-t)]
End NetLogo code

CONSUME-SUGAR-DELAYED runs this after I'm finished collecting sugar. DIE-IF-NO-SUGAR causes me to die if my-sugar becomes non-positive. INITIALISE-SUGAR-CONSUMPTION initialises my-sugar-consumption.

This decrements my-sugar by sugar consumption rate.

Based upon page 24 of [1].

This was implemented by Ken Kahn.

[1] Growing Artificial Societies by Joshua M. Epstein and Robert Axtell, Brookings Institute Press, The MIT Press, 1996.
