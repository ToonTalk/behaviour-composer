I turn towards a goal.

Begin micro-behaviour

TURN-TOWARDS-GOAL

Begin NetLogo code: 
substitute-text-area-for the-goal 10 10 
substitute-text-area-for cycle-duration 1          
do-every cycle-duration
   [let desired-heading heading-towards the-goal
    let desired-turn desired-heading - my-heading
    turn-right desired-turn]
End NetLogo code

Variants

The 10 10 represents the upper right corner. You can replace it with an attribute or the result of some computation (e.g. a random distribution). You may want to introduce a maximum turning speed.

Related Micro-behaviours

MOVE-FORWARD can be used together with this to move towards a goal. TURN-TOWARDS-ANOTHER turns me towards another individual.

How this works

I computes the angle from where I am to the x and y coordinates. I turn towards the goal by the difference between my desired-heading and my-heading.
