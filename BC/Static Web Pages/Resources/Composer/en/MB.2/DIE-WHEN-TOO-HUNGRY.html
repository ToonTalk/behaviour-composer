When my hunger exceeds 100, I die.

This behaviour illustrates the use of attributes (hunger in this case) and conditionals (when in this case).

Begin micro-behaviour

DIE-WHEN-TOO-HUNGRY

Begin NetLogo code: 
substitute-text-area-for maximum-hunger 100  
when [my-hunger > maximum-hunger]
     [add-behaviours list-of-micro-behaviours "Behaviours when too hungry" [DIE.html]]
End NetLogo code

WHEN and ADD-BEHAVIOURS are the BehaviourComposer's enhancements to NetLogo.

Variants

The attribute (hunger) and level (100) may be changed. Or the entire condition can be replaced with another condition. Other behaviours (other than DIE) can be triggered.

Related Micro-behaviours

DIE-WHEN-TOO-OLD is similar but the condition uses my-age rather than my-hunger.

How this works

When my value of my-hunger exceeds 100 then the DIE behaviour is added to me.
