I produce pollution due to sugar consumption.

Begin micro-behaviour

SUGAR-CONSUMPTION-PRODUCES-POLLUTION

Begin NetLogo code: 
do-after 50.003
   [do-every 1
       [set next-pollution-of-patch
            next-pollution-of-patch +
            my-sugar-consumption * my-pollution-due-to-consumption]]
End NetLogo code

Variants

This delays the onset of pollution by 50 time units -- other delays are possible.

Related Micro-behaviours

INITIALISE-POLLUTION-DUE-TO-CONSUMPTION-RATE initialises my-pollution-due-to-consumption and INITIALISE-SUGAR-CONSUMPTION initialises my-sugar-consumption. pollution-of-patch is used to influence where agents move in PREFER-PATCH-WITH-GREATER-SUGAR-TO-POLLUTION-RATIO.

How this works

After a delay this repeatedly increases the next value of pollution-of-patch of the patch where I am. The increment is the product of my sugar consumption and my pollution rate. It is delayed an addition .003 time units so this runs after sugar is consumed.
