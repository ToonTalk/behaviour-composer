I schedule the UPDATE-LOCATION micro-behaviour to happen after other micro-behaviours.

Begin micro-behaviour

UPDATE-LOCATION-DELAYED

Begin NetLogo code: 
do-after .004
   [add-behaviour UPDATE-LOCATION]
End NetLogo code

Variants

You can delay by any amount of time. Here we delay by .004 time units.

Related Micro-behaviours

UPDATE-LOCATION does the real work.

How this works

It schedules UPDATE-LOCATION to start .004 time units from now. This ensures that all other processes will have updated my-heading before doing this.
