You can constrain the position a rectangle to connect to another rectangle using the following. You can also change the colour of rectangles.

You can make one rectangle's position depend upon the position of another:

My bottom is always joined to another's top.

My top is always joined to another's bottom.

My left side is always joined to another's right side.

My right side is always joined to another's left side.

My center is always exactly above or below the center of another.

My center is always exactly the left or right of the center of another.
